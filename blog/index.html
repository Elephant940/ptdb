About me and this blog
Me at The Secret World of Elephants exhibition at the American Museum of Natural History.
I'm Trent Grasso, and I've been fascinated with elephants, mammoths, mastodons, and their relatives (collectively known as proboscideans) since I was a toddler. I created the Proboscidean Text Database to more formally organize the many different elephant-related sources I'd collected over the years, both for my own convenience and in the hopes it'd be useful to others with a similar passion. Through this blog, I want to use these sources to take an in-depth look at seemingly surface-level questions about elephants and their kin, exploring the history of a given topic and providing layman-friendly explanations of relevant concepts as I go. I hope you'll enjoy learning about elephants by reading this blog as much as I do by writing it. Feel free to email me at tjgrasso940@gmail.com with any elephant-related questions or comments.
